This chili recipes is the outcome of many experiments. Feel free to improvise heavily, I rarely stick to this 100%

Ingredients

  • Lentils
  • Carrots
  • Black beans
  • Kidney beans
  • Bell peppers
  • Canned diced tomatoes
  • Chili peppers:
    • Jalap
    • Poblano
    • Whatever they have that's spicy
  • Sweet potato

Spice mix

  • Chili powder
  • Cumin
  • Paprika
  • Onion powder
  • Garlic powder
  • White pepper
  • Cayanne pepper
  • Salt
  • Brown sugar
  • Dried oregano

Crispy Sweet Potatoes

  1. Dice into small 1/2inch cubes
  2. Toss the cubes in some freestyle spices
  3. Crisp them up in the oven for 30 minutes, turn them a few times

Chili Steps

  1. Simmer lentils for 30 minutes
  2. Get the onions in the pot for 5 minutes
  3. Add garlic for 1 minute
  4. Add the carrots
  5. Bell peppers IN
  6. Add the spice mix to the pot and toast them
  7. Add the tinned tomatoes
  8. Add all the beans and lentils (now drained)
  9. Add broth as appropriate
  10. Low boil for an hour or so
  11. Serve with the crispy sweet potatoes and enjoy with lots of cheese
