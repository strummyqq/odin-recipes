Enchilada recipe I wish I made more often. They are objectively the best anyone has ever eaten.

Ingredients

  • Tofu
  • Sweet potato
  • Red bell pepper
  • Chili peppers:
    • Jalap
    • Poblano
    • Whatever they have that's spicy
  • Cheese
  • Tortillas

Red Sauce ingredients

  • White flour
  • Tomato paste
  • Broth
  • Spice mix:
    • Chili powder
    • Cumin
    • Paprika
    • Onion powder
    • Garlic powder
    • Dried oregano

Enchiladas Steps

  1. Press and cut the tofu into bit-size cubes
  2. Toss the cubes in some cornstarch and spices
  3. Cube the sweet potato to similar size and toss in spices
  4. Get the tofu and sweet potato cubes in the oven

Make the Red Sauce

  1. Toast the white flour and spices in a saucepan for a few minute
  2. Make a hole in the middle of the pan and add all the tomato paste
  3. Whisk constantly for 15 minutes while slowly adding the broth
  4. After 15 minutes fold in some cheese and let it melt
  5. Mix the tofu and sweet potato with 1/2 of the red sauce

Construct and bake

  1. Use a deep pan to assemble enchilada by placing 1/8 of the filling
  2. Compact the 8 enchiladas side-by-side in the pan
  3. Pour the remaining red sauce over the top of the enchiladas and sprinkle on cheese
  4. Bake for 20 minutes then eat them all too fast
